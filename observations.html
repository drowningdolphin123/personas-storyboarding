Most residents, when using the machines, will:

  1. Approach the machines and look for one with an open door.
    • If there are no doors open, they will find one that says "Open Door" on the display signaling that the washer cycle is done and the previous user has yet to remove their clothes.
    • They will then either wait for the previous user to come and remove the laundry or they will remove it themselves to use the machine.
  2. Once finding (or making) an open machine, they will put their clothes in.
  3. They will then put in detergent.
    • If they use liquid or powder detergent, they will first open the soap tray. Some users free-hand the amount of detergent they use, others carefully measure out the detergent. Either way, they fill up the tray a certain amount, then close it to move on to the next step.
    • If they use tide-pods, users will toss in the tide pod(s) on their clothes before moving on to the next step.
    • In both instances, there are some users who use "too much soap".
  4. Once putting in the detergent, users will close the door to the machine and begin choosing their
    settings
    Wash Regular Deluxe Ultra
    Cycle Normal Delicate
    Temp Cold Warm Hot Start
    .
  5. After choosing the washer preferences, the users will press start and get ready to leave.
    • Some users will set a timer for the cycle length so they can time when they need to return to flip their laundry.
    • Some users will leave their laundry baskets in the laundry room, others take their baskets with them.
